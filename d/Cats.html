CATS

Characteristics

They are cute,funny,cool,violent sometimes, but overall they rock! Though do not underestimate their cute eyes. They are strong beautiful creatures.

Food

These precious cats need kibble, but you have to figure out what kind they like. Don't forget the treats though! I own a cat myself and she's really picky. Make sure to get to know your cat!

Cleaning

Cats can clean themselves but still once in a year or two you should probably wash them, especially if you let them roam in your backyard. Roaming in your backyard would probably allow ticks to climb on to their fur, NOT GOOD! Though daily you should probably use a cat brush and brush their fur.
