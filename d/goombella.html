All About Kaedehara Kazuha

Best Artifact set for Kazuha

Four-Piece Viridescent Venerer
Viridescent Venerer is Kazuha’s best artifact set for any build. A two-piece buffs his Anemo DMG by 15 percent, which boosts the damage he does with his skill
and burst. A four-piece Viridescent Venerer boosts Swirl DMG by 60 percent and lowers enemies’ Elemental RES to the element infused in the Swirl by 40 percent.

Best Weapon for Kazuha

Freedom Sworn was basically made for Kazuha. It raises Elemental Mastery up to 198 and enables Elemental Reactions with its ability. You can trigger the "Millenial
Movement: Song of Resistance," When the weapon wielder triggers two Elemental Reaction, which increases ATK by 20 percent and Normal, Charged, and Plunging
Attack DMG by 16 percent. In short, Kazuha should have no problem taking advantage of these significant buffs with how easy it is for him to trigger reactions.

Best Team Comps for Kazuha

Kazuha works best in teams that benefit from Swirl DMG. Swirl DMG spreads Elemental Reactions and increases the Elemental DMG from them. With an element like
Pyro, that means boosting the already existing Elemental DMG from the reaction into something more devastating. In that sense, Kazuha complements a DPS that
liberally applies Elemental DMG like Hu Tao, Xiangling, or Yanfei.
