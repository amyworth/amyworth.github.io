All About Husky

Food!

They eat raw meat, fruits, vegetable. Types of raw meat they eat are chicken,beef, lamb, fish.Types of fruit they eat are all fruit except grapes,prunes,and raisins.They can eat all vegetables.

Fun Facts About Huskies

Huskies were made in Russia,imported because of a Gold Rush. They can hear 10 miles away. They are best known for their incredible sled pulling skills. They love to run, pull sleds, digging holes, and love stuffed animals.Below 75 degree Farenheit is about hot for them. Huskies are the 74th smartest dogs. Huskies can only live 12-15 years. Huskies can cost up to 800-1,500 dollars.
