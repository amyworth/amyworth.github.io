Wendy's History

When was Wendy's established?

Wendy's was established in 1969 by Dave Thomas. Wendy's is a international fast food chain. The title for the third largest hamburger chain belongs to them.

The Baconator and Baconator's son

The Baconator has, obviously, bacon, ground beef chunk, salt, mayonnnaise, and american cheese slices. The baconator has all the exact same ingredients except for a smaller patty for the beef. You have to try it, if you haven't already.
