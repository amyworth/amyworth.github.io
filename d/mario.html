All about wolves

What they eat

Wolves are carnivores which means they only eat meat such as deer, elk, moose, caribou, and bison.They eat all of their meat raw.When they catch prey,alpha wolves get to eat first.

Environment

Wolves mostly live in North America, Europe, Asia and North Africa.They live where there are animals to catch and eat. Most wolves today are found in the frozen tundras in Canada and Alaska, where there aren't many people or animals to bother them.

Characteristics

Wolves mostly are found in packs of 2-10.They are caring and playful to other wolves.Wolves are also very intelligent because they educate their young, take care of their injured and live in family groups.They have thick fur to stand in the cold.
