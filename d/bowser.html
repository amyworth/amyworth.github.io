All about the six yonkos

White Beard

White beard has been a yonko the longest and is also the strongest yonko.White Beard ate the Gura Gura no Mi fruit which is the strongest devil fruit.Black Beard and his crew killed him.

Shanks

Shanks is the captain of the red haired pirates.Shanks conquered haki and has no devil fruit.Shanks wore a straw hat but gave it to luffy and told him to give it back to him when he becomes a great pirate.

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy is the captain of the straw hat pirates. he ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi fruit.Luffy was borned with conquered haki.Luffy wants to king of pirates.
