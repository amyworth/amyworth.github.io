Rules of Basketball

  • There can be only five players per team on the court.
  • Score more than your opponent.
  • Score within the shot clock.
  • Dribbling advances the ball.
  • The offense has five seconds to inbound the ball.
  • The offense must advance the ball.
  • Ball and ballhander must remain inbounds.
