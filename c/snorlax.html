All About Clown Fish

Food

They eat fish eggs, larvae, copepods, algae, worms, and small crustareans.

Live

The Atlantic Ocean meets the Indian Ocean south of Africa at Cape Agulhas.
The Indian Ocean, the third largest, extends northward from the Southern Ocean to India,
the Arabian Peninsula, and Southeast Asia in Asia, and between Africa in the west and Australia
in the east.

What Are Clownfishes

A small tropical marine fish with bold vertical stripes or other bright coloration.
It lives in close association with anemones and is protected from their stings by mucus.
Also called anemone fish.
