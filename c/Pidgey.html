All About Cheetahs

Strenght

Cheetahs are very strong. They are apex predators engineered to stalk and kil.Cheetahs have a bite thats five hundred PSI.They also have a strike strong enough to kill or fight off a warthog or an antelope. Cheetahs are three times stronger than humans but weaker than other big cats.

Behavior

Adult cheetahs may be found alone or in all male groups called a coaltions. A coalition of male cheetahs — most likely two to three litter mates that have stayed together — defend a territory against other males, rarely but sometimes to the death. Coalition membership may last for the entire life of the male.

new born cub!

At birth, the cubs weigh 8.5 to 15 ounces and are blind and helpless. Their mother will groom them patiently, purring quietly and providing them warmth and security. After a day or so, the mother will leave the cubs to hunt for herself, so she can continue to care for the cubs.
